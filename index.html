Instructions

How to play:

  • Survive by attacking and Avoiding the enemy characters, You Have 5 Lives
  • Fire Balls, Dinosaur, Dragon are the enemy characters
  • Kill enemies by attacking them, But you can not attack Fire balls
  • If you kill the Dinosaur, the position of the Dragon will be changed

Controls:

  • On-screen buttons
  • Keyboard arrows and WASD: Move left and right, jump, and duck
  • Press Space to function the Kill

Special Note:

  • The game has been designed for desktop devices with full screen mode
  • So please make sure to get a better experience
